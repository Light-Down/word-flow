2. What data we collect and why

2.1 Account & authentication

To use Wordflow, you sign in with your email address via a Magic Link (passwordless login). When you do so, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Date and time of account creation and last login
  • Trial start date and licence status (trial / purchased)
  • Device information: device name, anonymised device ID, app version, macOS version, last seen timestamp

Purpose: To authenticate you, manage your trial period, and deliver licence status to the app.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — necessary to provide the service you requested.
Retention: For the duration of your account. You can request deletion at any time.

2.2 Purchase data

If you purchase a Wordflow licence, the payment is processed by Gumroad (see Section 3). We receive a confirmation from Gumroad containing:

  • Your email address (used to activate your licence)
  • Purchase confirmation and licence type

Purpose: To activate your licence and link it to your account.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — necessary to fulfil the purchase contract.
Retention: As required by German tax law (§ 147 AO), typically 10 years.

2.3 Feedback submissions

If you submit feedback via the app or website, we may collect:

  • Your feedback message
  • Optionally: your email address (if you choose to provide it)
  • App version number

Purpose: To improve the product.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in improving the service.
Retention: Until no longer needed for product development.

2.4 Data we do NOT collect

  • No cookies are set on this website
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used on this website
  • No audio recordings — your voice data goes directly from your device to Groq's API via your own API key. Wordflow's backend only handles authentication and licence status, never audio or transcription content.

3. Third-party services

Supabase (authentication & backend)

We use Supabase (Supabase Inc., 970 Toa Payoh North, Singapore) to manage user accounts, send Magic Link login emails, and store licence data. Your email address and licence information are stored on Supabase's infrastructure, hosted in the EU (Frankfurt, AWS eu-central-1).

Supabase's privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

A data processing agreement (DPA) is in place with Supabase as required by Art. 28 GDPR.

Gumroad (payment processing)

Payments for the Wordflow licence are processed by Gumroad (Gumroad, Inc., 548 Market Street, Suite 72906, San Francisco, CA 94104, USA). Gumroad acts as the Merchant of Record. Your payment details are handled exclusively by Gumroad — we do not receive or store your credit card or bank information.

Gumroad's privacy policy: gumroad.com/privacy

Groq (in-app, not this website)

The Wordflow app uses the Groq API for transcription and text processing. This connection is established using your own Groq API key — no audio or transcription data passes through Wordflow's servers. Groq's privacy policy applies directly between you and Groq: groq.com/privacy-policy

Google Fonts & CDN resources

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts and a Tailwind CSS CDN. These requests may transmit your IP address to Google and Tailwind's CDN provider. We plan to self-host these resources in a future update to eliminate this transfer.

4. Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@olenberg-media.de. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. The responsible authority for Frankfurt am Main is:
Der Hessische Beauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit (HBDI)
datenschutz.hessen.de

5. Data security

We use technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Authentication data and licence information are stored on Supabase's infrastructure in the EU (Frankfurt). Access to user data is protected by row-level security policies and JWT-based authentication. No passwords are stored — login is exclusively via one-time Magic Links sent to your email address.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this URL. Material changes will be communicated via the email address you provided at sign-up.
